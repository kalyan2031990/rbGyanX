IMPORTANT — CLINICAL USE

rbGyanX provides decision support for radiotherapy and radiosurgery plan
evaluation. It is NOT a substitute for qualified clinical judgment.

• Outputs (TCP, NTCP, UTCP, QUANTEC flags, ML predictions) are for
  research and decision support only unless validated for your local workflow.
• Machine-learning features require adequate outcome data; follow in-app
  safety annotations in ADVANCED mode.
• De-identify patient data according to your institution's policies.

By installing this software you agree to use it responsibly and in
compliance with applicable regulations at your site.
